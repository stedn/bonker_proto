This is just a short post about a hallway wall decoration I made over the weekend. It's supposed to resemble a network graph, linking the different pieces together.

Basically I had a lot of unrelated stuff to put on our last empty wall and I needed a clever way to get it all on there. I hope you find it as amusing as I do.
