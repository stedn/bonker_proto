Why call this site bonkerfield?

This site is my best attempt to catalog the information-entity known as Will Stedden. It obviously has short-comings; it doesn't even begin to capture every antecedent, and it's clearly an editorialization of only the information outflow that I choose to showcase. Why did you choose word bonkerfield?

Originally, bonkerfield was just a funny word my partner used to describe anything in the extreme (eg "It's hotter than a bonkerfield in here"). Mostly she used it to describe me when I was being crazy.

I was trying to think of a good word for the information-identity spacetime field, and I realized it was probably the craziest thing I've ever imagined. And then, I realized that a bonkerfield should be some kind of field anyway.
