We are living in what might be the most frivolous and ironic of times, but we can change that. Join in a fight for sincerity. We deserve a better "About" page.

Below I've laid out my sincere reasons for being Here. I've articulated my views on what makes up my self, the purpose of existing, and how I should act. I hope you can appreciate this effort, and that you consider your reasons that motivate you too.

Because "because reasons" isn't reason enough for me anymore.

My identity is composite and continuous

When I say "me" I'm not talking about an atomic individual or a subjective internal mind. When I say "me," I mean an amorphous continuum of streams of information, a collection of inputs, the state of an information transforming machine, and all of its outputs.

I must believe the universe has a purpose

I have derived a thought experiment that, taken with a few premises, leads to the belief that the universe has a purpose. I satisfy those premises, and so I believe the universe has a purpose.

The proper course of action must come from intuition

Importantly, just because I accept that the universe has a purpose, this doesn't mean that I know it's purpose. Without knowing the purpose, I cannot derive a clear course of action to achieve the purpose. Again, using another thought experiment, I've concluded that I must follow my intuitions as to what is the correct course of action to help achieve the purpose of the universe.

I can only optimizing over a multitude of intuitions

My intuitions are composite, impermanent, and hard to pin down, and yet they are all that's left to tell me what to do. I can never clearly know what is best to do so I can only choose my actions from an expansive probabilistic function that I will never be able to know with certainty.

So this is what I do.

What do you do?
